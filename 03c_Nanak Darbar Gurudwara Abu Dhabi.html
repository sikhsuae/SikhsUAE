Gurudwara Guru Nanak Darbar Ji, Abu Dhabi

Gurudwara Nanak Darbar is one of the established Sikh places of worship in Abu Dhabi, located within the city in Al Manhal residential area.

The Abu Dhabi government allocated land for it in 2009, and it was officially inaugurated in 2012. Its architecture combines modern design with traditional Sikh elements such as domes and arches.

The Gurudwara serves as a key spiritual and community hub for Sikhs in the capital.

The gurudwara hosts daily prayers, langar, and major Sikh religious celebrations throughout the year.
